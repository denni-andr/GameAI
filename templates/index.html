Game Tutorial

Chain Reaction is a deterministic combinatorial game involving atleast 2 players.

A player can place an 'orb' of his color in any cell. They can also be placed on an already filled cell to form an orb of size 2. A single cell can accomdate more than one orb.

However the orb bursts in all 4 directions when it reaches the maximum amount and thus taking any opponent orbs in the surrounding as its own and also increasing the orb count.

The orb bursts open on placing

  • 2 orbs in the corner
  • 3 orbs in the edge
  • 4 orbs in the center

When a orb bursts open, it can cause a chain reaction to occur, where orbs keep on bursting open, spreading everywhere.

The game is won by a player if he occupies all cells of the board.
